"College For All"

With Sander's College for All plan he hopes to provide free tuition at all public colleges and universities. In the plan, the federal government would pay for two-thirds of the cost of public college tuition while state governments will have to pick up the remaining third. Sanders also hopes to cut student loan interest rates a little over 2 percent, and tie interest rates to inflation.
