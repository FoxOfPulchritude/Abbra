His Plan

Christie wants rich individuals and hedge funds to invest in students' college tuitions. Investors would pay a student's entire tuition and then the student would give the investor a small percentage of their annual income for a set period of time. The example, provided by Rubio, was an investor paying a $10,000 tuition and the student giving the investor four percent of their annual income after college for ten years. Marco Rubio also supports this plan.
