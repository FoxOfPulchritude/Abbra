His Plan

Rubio wants rich individuals and hedge funds to invest in students' college tuitions. Investors would pay a student's entire tuition and then the student would give the investor a small percentage of their annual income for a set period of time. The example Rubio provided was an investor paying a $10,000 tuition and the student giving the investor four percent of their annual income after college for ten years. Chris Christie also supports this plan.

Noteworthy Info

Rubio is also a supporter of the "Student RIght to Know Before You Go" act. This act says that a university must provide information to potential students that could affect their choice to go to that school. The necessary information includes: post-graduation average annual income. Rates of remedial enrollment, credit accumulation, and graduation. Average cost of the program and average debt accumulated.
